Biography

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel (1875–1950), also known as the "Iron Man of India," was a key figure in India’s struggle for independence and the country’s first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister. He played a pivotal role in integrating over 500 princely states into the Indian Union after independence, ensuring the political unity and integrity of India.

“Manpower without Unity is not a strength unless it is harmonized and united properly, then it becomes a spiritual power.”
